About Us

Found2Edu (F2E) is a platform for educational dissemination and scholarship consulting that was founded in 2020 - we share and provide consulting to domestic students seeking to access and pursue an excellent quality education through both domestic and international scholarships. By acting as a link between scholarship seekers and scholarship providers, we intend to increase the accessibility of "an excellent quality education" for all Lao people. With this view in mind, we believe “If you dream it, you can do it.”

We can show you how to be a strong candidate in your application, essay, and interview. Furthermore, our staff can assist you in identifying and improving your skills so that you have a better chance of receiving a scholarship award.
